Introduction:
> Blackjack is a simple card game made popular by casinos.
> The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
> The value of a player's hand is the sum of the points of all the cards in the player's hand.
> This means that the person with points closest to but not above 21 wins the game.
Points:
> Each face card(i.e. K, Q and J) is of 10 points each.
> All other cards execpt Ace are worth their numeric value itself.
> An Ace can be counted as either 1 or 11 points according to the player's wish.
> In this specific program, the computer automaticaly chooses the best value for an Ace.
Gameplay:
> Each player is dealt 2 cards at the beginning.
> The player can then 'HIT' or 'STAND'.
> To hit, means to ask the dealer for another card.
> To stand, means to ask for no more cards and end your turn.
> A 'BUST' happens if the points of a player exceeds 21.
> A 'BUSTED' player immediately looses the game.
Blackjack:
> A 'BLACKJACK' is a combination of 2 cards that perfectly adds upto 21 i.e. one card of
ten points and an Ace.
> The player with a blackjack immediatley wins the game.
